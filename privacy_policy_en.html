Privacy Policy

Eho Compass
Last Updated: November 26, 2025

"Eho Compass" (hereinafter referred to as "the App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled in the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Location Information (Compass Data)

The App uses your device's compass functionality to display the lucky direction (Eho).

Important:
  • Location information collected is used solely for display on the app screen
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • No data is saved on your device
  • No data is shared with third parties

1.2 Other Information

The App does NOT collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personally identifiable information
  • Usage history
  • Device information
  • Any other personal information

2. Purpose of Information Use

The location information collected is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Displaying the direction your device is facing
  • Calculating and displaying the difference from the lucky direction (Eho)

3. Information Sharing with Third Parties

The App does not provide, disclose, or share your information with any third parties.

4. Advertising and Analytics Tools

The App does not display advertisements or perform usage analytics. Therefore, no third-party advertising services or analytics tools are used.

5. Security

Since the App does not transmit user information externally, there is no risk of information leakage. All processing is completed within your device.

6. Children's Privacy

The App does not intentionally collect information from children under 13 years of age. Furthermore, the App does not have functionality to collect personal information.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as necessary. Changes to this Privacy Policy will become effective when posted on this page.
