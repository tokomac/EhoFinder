About Eho (ÊÅµÊñπ)

Eho is a traditional Japanese concept referring to an auspicious direction that changes each year according to the ancient Chinese zodiac calendar. During Setsubun (the day before spring begins in the lunar calendar, usually February 3rd), it's customary to eat a sushi roll called "Eho-maki" while facing the lucky direction for that year. This tradition is believed to bring good fortune and prosperity.
